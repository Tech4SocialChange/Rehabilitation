Emergency Info

Emergency Contact number and Processes

Fire Service:

Contact number 1: 029555555
Contact number 2: 029556666
There are two process to contact with Fire Service.
Process 1:
  • Neighbors Call directly to the number above.
  • Tell the operator how to reach the Exact location, Address Details, Routes etc.
  • Fire service has smaller vehicle to enter a narrow roads or lane so it is important to tell the operator about the narrow lane or roads to reach at the fire points.
  • Contact person from the neighbor make sure his/her phone is always open in terms of contact with the fire service operator.
Process 2:
  • Contact person from the location can call the trained volunteer/Leader assigned from fire service who lives that area.
  • They come first before the fire car arrived at the location, they starts minimize the fire with their equipment first.

Police Emergency:

Police Control Room: 027124000
Mirpur Pallabi Thana: 029556666
RAB 4 (Rapid Action Battalion): 01777710499
Process
  • If any occurrence or crime take place, Like murder, Robbing, Snatching, Suicide, Political Clash, Illegal Drug Supply, Violence against women and Child, Land Resources Conflicts, anyone can contact/call to this number. After that law enforcement agency can take action.
  • Otherwise every wards of Dhaka city has it own Local Community members. General people also can contact with them and they contact to the law enforcement agency and take action.
